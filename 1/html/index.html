About us

Established over a century ago as a government school, this institution transitioned to private management in 1961-62 under the stewardship of Thiru V.S Thyagaraja Mudaliar, in honor of his adoptive father, Vadathimangalam Thiru V.T Somasundaram. Since then, it has flourished, evolving into a prestigious higher secondary school in 1978-79. Recognizing excellence, retired teachers are celebrated with meaningful mementos, while top-performing students in grades 10-12 receive accolades sponsored by various trusts. With visionary leadership, dedicated staff, and robust support from the community, this institution, boasting 2500 students and 100 teachers, maintains a sterling reputation in the district. With a focus on holistic development, it fosters athletic prowess through daily physical training and participation in diverse sports, facilitated by ample facilities including a spacious playground. Amidst lush greenery, this school stands as a beacon of educational excellence and holistic growth, symbolizing a century-old legacy of commitment to nurturing both mind and body.

Our Institution

The VS Boys Higher Secondary School and Sri GRM Girls Higher Secondary School are esteemed institutions with a legacy of excellence in education. Founded over a century ago, these schools have evolved under visionary leadership and dedicated staff. Recognizing academic and athletic achievements, they honor both students and retired teachers, fostering a culture of appreciation and growth. With robust support from the community and a commitment to holistic development, these institutions stand as pillars of educational excellence, shaping future leaders and contributing to the progress of society.

V.S BOYS HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL

The V.S Boys Higher Secondary School is a venerable institution with a rich history of educational excellence. Established over a century ago, it began as a government school before transitioning to private management in 1961-62 under the leadership of Thiru V.S Thyagaraja Mudaliar, in honor of his adoptive father, Vadathimangalam Thiru V.T Somasundaram.

SRI.G.RANGANATHA MUDALIAR HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL

Sri GRM Girls Higher Secondary School is an esteemed educational institution known for its dedication to empowering young women through quality education and holistic development opportunities. Established with a mission to nurture intellect, character, and leadership skills, the school has emerged as a beacon of excellence in the region.
